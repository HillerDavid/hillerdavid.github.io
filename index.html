About Me

My name is David Hiller. I am a proud disney cast member, advocate of all things nerd culture, and future full stack web developer. Currently I am enrolled in UCF coding bootcamp in partnership with Trilogy Education Services. Within our curriculum, I have obtained skills in HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery and Node.js. Looking to the future, I am excited to contribute my skills as a web developer to the other areas of my life.


Disney has always had a special place in my life growing up, as it has in many other people. The magic in Disney has been it's ability to create happiness. As a cast member of Walt Disney World, I have the privilege to create that magic. Making magic for others by being a part of the web development team would be a dream opportunity.


Video games, comics, board games, anime are all among my various hobbies and interests.
